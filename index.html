Some projects I've worked on recently...

ml+aging

Using machine learning to predict stem cell aging.

- Obtained blood stem cell gene expression dataset
- Trained multiple supervised classifier models to identify young or old
- Selected critical subset of genes that predicted age with 98.5% accuracy

Currently exploring the therapeutic and rejuvenative implications of the study. Work is done under the Stanford CS department, and the paper is available upon request.

3D image analysis

Building a workflow for 3D fluorescent microscopy analysis.

While in the lab, I realized that there were few tools to quantify data present in microscopic images.

- Created a workflow for analyzing 3D images from fluorescent microscopy
- Given a surface rendering, relationships can be quantified (distance, density, and num branch points)

The tool increased experimental precision and reproducibility, and was quickly adopted by the lab. The project was sponsored by Longaker Lab and Stanford Stem Cell Institute. Our manuscript is under review.
